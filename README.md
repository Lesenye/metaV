# metaV
MetaV is an app designed for users to get their suggestions of the day amd to remind them of what must be done during their dail living,
The app is built to help people get quick ideas and suggestions so users have a reminder of what has to be done throughout their day.
MetaV instruction video : https://youtu.be/ueM7ang8_aw

Open the Meta V app on your mobile home screen Enter your time of day to get our suggestion eg.(Morning,Midday,Afternoon,Evening,Night),If non of the above examples times have been entered you mabe get "Invalid Input".
